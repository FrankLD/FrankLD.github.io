A Tale of Two Cities

About

Born in Boston, raised in Massachusetts, I’m here in New York City after graduating from a prominent college in Bolivia, South America. After spending the past 9 years abroad, I have finally made my way back home with new ideas, dreams and a hard-working attitude. I am eager to further my knowledge and establish a career in Graphic and Web Design, all while also pursuing acting, improv and music.


I hold a bachelor degree in Graphic Design and Visual Communication. An education in Bolivia and the U.S. afforded me the opportunity to navigate constantly in between two different languages and cultures. To me, this gives me the advantage. I am able to thrive in diverse and complex environments and understand well the sensibilities of different cultural aesthetics. I’m open to learning and listening. I can create thoughtful and mindful designs informed by these real life complexities.
